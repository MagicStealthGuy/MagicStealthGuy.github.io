Zuki Vikuli's Relationship Guide

Friendship Levels

0 Points: Zuki wants you dead. He doesn’t wish death on most ponies, so you must’ve actively pissed him off to reach this point.
1 Point: Zuki hates you and will not be shy about letting you know. He will actively avoid or be aggressive with you at nearly every turn.
2 Points: Zuki dislikes you. He will actively ignore you, act condescending, and object to your thoughts.
3 Points: Zuki has a general disdain for you. He will be condescending towards you and not take you seriously.
4 Points: Zuki is slightly bothered by your behavior. He is more likely to sass you or criticize your actions.
5 Points: Neutral. Zuki will be more than polite with you as he is with everypony. He does not dislike you.
6 Points: Zuki considers you a friend. He will confide in you his personal feelings in good faith.
7 Points: Zuki really likes you. He will pray for your well being, trust your words, and participate in most activities involving you.
8 Points: Best Friends. At this point Zuki trusts you enough to be emotionally vulnerable with you. He willingly lets his guard down around you and accepts comfort instead of giving it.
9 Points: Zuki considers you his second family. He has an active and vested interest in your life and safety, and will actively fight tooth and hoof to protect you.
10 Points: Zuki will die for you. Your life in his eyes is as important as his mother’s life, his homeland, his princess. He shows a love and care for you so genuine that it could move mountains.

Love Levels

0 Points: Zuki does not have any strong feelings for you.
1 Point: Zuki has a small attachment to your well-being.
2 Points: Zuki cares about you more than a friend but does not know how to feel about it.
3 Points: Zuki is aware that he cares about you more than a friend and may actively worry for you.
4 Points: Zuki is likely to be flustered by your intimate advances.
5 Points: Zuki enjoys spending time with you and would like to spend a good amount of his personal time with you.
6 Points: Zuki will get protective over you.
7 Points: Zuki will actively fight for your safety and happiness without question and will not be shy about his own intimate advances.
8 Points: Zuki loves you dearly and is proud to say it. He sees you as a commited partner in his eyes.
9 Points: Zuki is seriously considering commiting to you as if you were family.
10 Points: Zuki will lay down his life in your name. Your life is his everything and cannot bear the thought of being without you. He will bring the world to your doorstep if you ask it of him.
